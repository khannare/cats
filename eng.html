What Is an Energetic Personality?

People with a high energy score are energetic, enthusiastic, and effervescent. They're gregarious, competitive, sociable, and like having a good time. They are more willing to meet new people, see new places, and do new things.


Which Cat Suits An Energetic Personality?

The Japanese bobtail would be the best. After a luxurious existence as royalty's pets, the Japanese bobtail was forced to work. Mice were threatening Japan's major source of revenue at the time, so the emperor sent them out into the woods to hunt them down. The "Cat Who Saved Japan" is how they're known. After recovering their rightful place as house kings and queens, the Japanese Bobtails have plenty of energy left over. They can play fetch or tag if there are other cats in the house.


Facts About The Japanese Bobtail Breed.

  • The beluga cat or the polite cat meme is actually a Japanese Bobtail cat.
  • They grow up to 8-9 inches and weight 6-10 pounds.
  • They are good with children, seniors, dogs, cats and families in general.
  • Their life span can go up to 15 years.
  • In Japan the Japanese Bobtail represents good luck, which is known as the maneki-neko("beckoning cat")
