What Is An Independant Personality?

Individuals that are self-sufficient have their own thoughts. As a result of this characteristic, their beliefs and values are firmly set, and they are not easily swayed by the opinions of others. They make decisions based on their own views rather than social expectations or judgements.


Which Cat Breed Suits An Independant Personality?

You should get the Chartreux breed. They are a wonderful buddy to return home to for the go-getters and lone wolves. They prefer to be with their ownersbut they don't always cling to them. Whether they're cuddling with you or sleeping while you're at work, these cats are quiet, peaceful, and content.



Facts About The Chartreux Breed

  • The Chartreux is a talkative cat, most of them prefer to "chirp" rather than "meow."
  • Despite its bulk, the Chartruex enjoys playing and can do acrobatics.
  • They are a rare breed that originated in France.
