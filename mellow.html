What Is a Mellow Personality?

Mellow refers to someone or anything who is peaceful, smooth, and devoid of abrasions. Mellow is defined as a person who is calm and serene and does not quickly become irritated. Mellow coffee is defined as having a smooth, tranquil flavour that isn't harsh.


Which Cat Suits A Mellow Personality?

If snuggling up on the couch with a nice book is your idea of a good time, the British Shorthair is the pet for you. When you sit down to pet their soft hairdo, these cats will be just as happy as you are. Of all our breeds, they have the densest coat,. Shorthairs in the United Kingdom are calm and typically go with the flow. They don't require much in the way of care or fun, but don't be shocked if they start following you around the home.


Facts About The British Shorthair Breed

  • They have a broad chest, muscular neck, strong jaws and a well developed muzzle.
  • They're known for their easygoing attitude about life.
  • They are really smart, affectionate and don't have a temperamental side.
  • They don't like getting picked up. They would start scracthing and hissing at you, because they get all stiff when lifted.
  • They tend to shed most during fall and spring, when moulting is most prevalent.
